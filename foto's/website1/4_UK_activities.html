Activities/ Surroundings

Rødberg
Fjordgløtt Camping og Hytesenter is 3,73 miles south of the town Rødberg. In Rødberg you can do your daily shopping. In Rødberg are banks, super markets, gas stations, garages, post office etc..

Langedrag
At 15,53 miles from the campsite, you will find the nature park "Langedrag". In Langedrag (also see website www.langedrag.no) all the Norwegian animal sorts can be admired. There are reindeers, wolves, lynxes, mountain foxes, horses, wild boars and a lot more small animals. Here you can fully enjoy the beautiful flora and fauna that Norway has to offer. Would you like to go horse riding? This can be arranged at Langedrag!

Nore og Uvdal Bygdetun
Between Rødberg and Uvdal, at 9,32 miles from our campsite is 'Nore og Uvdal Bygedetun'. You can visit this garden with its many historic buildings and small barns which originally come from this area. A definite must here is the church from the 12th century, an original wooden church from which you may not miss the inside!

Dresin in Veggli
Do you like cycling and would you like to try something new? Between Veggli (18,64 miles south of Rødberg) and Rødberg runs an old railway track which was once part of the 'Numedalsbanen'. Cycling with a cart on the railroad, you will enjoy a wonderful cycling tour to Rødberg or you can also get off close to our campsite. Along the way you can enjoy the beautiful nature. And don't forget to bring a well filled, delicious pick nick basket! You can reserve the dresins at the campsite.

Rafting
In Dagli, 24,85 miles north of the Fjordgløtt Camping og Hytesenter, you make use of different rafting bookings. You can choose from 3 different adventures: normal, extreme and extreme plus. So there is always one adventure available for you!

Karting and Rally Racing
It is possible to rent karts, all terrain vehicles and rally cars close to Dagali Airport at Dagali Opplevelser. The whole year round you can make a booking for groups or individual bookings. The 'D.O.' is responsible for all the necessary safety meanings and works with certified instructors who are all active in car sports.

Fishing
In the area there a plenty possibilities to go fishing. Though be aware because you will need a Norwegian fish card ('fiskekort'). These cards can be bought e.g. at the campsite.
On a boat, fishing on the Norefjord lake is absolutely perfect. The Norefjord lake is begin fed from the 'Numedalslagen; a small river. From this small river you can easily fish for e.g. trout .

In Winter it is really nice to go ice fishing!

'Hardangervidda Nature Park'
When driving north from the E40, you will arrive at the 'Hardangervidda Nature park' This is the largest mountain plateau in Northern Europe (3.430km2). The 'Normannsslepa- path is an ancient path which runs from the east to the west plateau. Along this path there is a lot to be found from the early inhabitants. The 'Hardangervidda Nature park' is a perfect area to walk, mountain bike and of course in Winter to ski and cross ski.
