The Cabins:

In total there are 6 cabins on our campsite:
1x 6- 8 persons cabin
2x: 2 persons cabin
3x: 3-9 persons cabin

With the exception of the 2 persons cabins, all cabins are luxuriously equipped and perfectly suitable for a prolonged stay. All these cabins have a shower, toilet, kitchen, television, sitting area with a separate dining table and under floor heating.

The 3-6 persons cabins have 1 bedroom for 3 persons as well as a sofa bed in the living room. The 6-8 persons cabin has 2 bedrooms for 3 persons and an attic which suits 4 persons.

The kitchens are equipped with a refrigerator, hot plate, oven, cooker hood, automatic coffee maker and crockery. Furthermore the cabins are almost directly located at the fjord. From your own terrace you can enjoy the wonderful view of the Norefjord.

The 2- persons cabins are simply furnished with a bunk bed, sitting area, hot plate and a refrigerator.

You need to bring your own sheets, sleeping bags or duvets. Though you can also rent linen on the campsite.

Near all the cabins there is plenty of space to park one or more cars.
