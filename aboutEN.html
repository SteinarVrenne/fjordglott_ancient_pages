How do i get to Fjordgløtt Camping?



Campsites are located next to RV 40 (1.6 km), between Kongsberg and Geilo. From Kongsberg it is approx. 1 hour drive and from Geilo approx. 45 minutes. When you come from Oslo travel via Drammen and Kongsberg, approx. 2.5 hours. Fjordgløtt Camping is approx. 6 kilometers south from Rødberg. At the sign "Vrenne / Fjordgløtt Camping" you drive over the bridge and follow the road about 1.6 km.
